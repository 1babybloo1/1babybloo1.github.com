About Me

For many years now I have grown to love graphic design. I started off using 3D software (mainly Blender 3D) to create rendered graphics. I have practiced and honed the skill through the assistance of YouTube, professional advice and the internet, taking inspiration from other artists on websites such as Behance and Artstation.

I now have also touched upon different mediums, such as 2D graphics and illustration, brutalism and photography. I have taken upon experimenting with new styles, methods and principles of graphic design. I mostly enjoy creating brutalism posters. i love the grungy textures, bold shapes and the distinctive style and principles used to make the images.

My journey consists of six years of experimentation, practice and cups of decaffe coffee to get to where i am now, and have enjoyed every moment, and hope to do so for a long time.
